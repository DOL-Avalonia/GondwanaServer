# /Autoloot
Commands.Players.Autoloot.Description:	Automatically picks up loot around.
Commands.Players.Autoloot.Off:			Your autoloot is deactivated. You will no longer automatically pick up nearby loot.
Commands.Players.Autoloot.On:			Your autoloot is activated. You will now automatically pick up nearby loot.
Commands.Players.Autoloot.Usage:		Usage : '/autoloot <on|off>'
