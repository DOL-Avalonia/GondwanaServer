# GAMEFUNCATAPULT

GameFunCatapult.SeatOccupied:		The catapult seat is currently occupied.
GameFunCatapult.MissingItem:		You lack the required item to operate this catapult.
GameFunCatapult.KeyBreaks:			Your key breaks as you take control of the catapult.

GameFunCatapult.Launched:			You are launched by the catapult!
GameFunCatapult.Malfunction:		The catapult malfunctioned. (Spell ID not found)
