GuildRegistrar.Interact.RegisterMode.Line1: Hail. If you wish to found a guild, you must present a stamped Guild Register.
GuildRegistrar.Interact.RegisterMode.Line2: Have you come to [{0}]?
GuildRegistrar.Interact.RegisterMode.Line3: Or would you like to [{0}]?

GuildRegistrar.Whisper.Form.Normal.Line1: Gather {0} souls who would pledge to your cause and bring them before me.
GuildRegistrar.Whisper.Form.Normal.Line2: The tithe is one gold piece. Once paid, command: /gc form <guildname>.

GuildRegistrar.Whisper.Form.Register.Line1: Hand me the legalized register. The title shall be the guild name, and the founders must be inscribed within.
GuildRegistrar.Whisper.Form.Register.Line2: Any founder named in the text may present the document.
GuildRegistrar.Whisper.Form.Register.Line3: Would you like to [{0}]?

GuildRegistrar.Receive.NotRequired:     By the new laws, a register is not required. Gather your founders and use /gc form <guildname>.
GuildRegistrar.Receive.NotBook:         This is not a proper register book. Provide a valid document.

GuildRegistrar.Receive.NotRegister:     This document is not a Guild Register. I require the proper paperwork.
GuildRegistrar.Receive.NotStamped:      This register lacks the Treasury Seal. Bring me a *stamped* Guild Register.
GuildRegistrar.Receive.InvalidName:     This register title cannot be used as a guild name. It violates our naming protocols.
GuildRegistrar.Receive.NameTaken:       That guild name is already claimed by another order.
GuildRegistrar.Receive.IncompleteList:  The register is incomplete; the list of founders is lacking.
GuildRegistrar.Receive.NotFounder:      Only a founder named within the register may present it for finalization.
